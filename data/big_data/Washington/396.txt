Driving in my car on the highway, my friend and I saw a dark figure ahead of us walking up on a bluff beside the road. He was walking towards us. As we got very near we saw him clearly. A hairy tall man like figure about 7 to 8 ft. tall. He was very dark, and his face was very clear. His head had a peak to it. He turned and looked at our car and then went off into the trees to his left. we both looked at each other and confirmed that we had just seen big foot! It was amazing.
